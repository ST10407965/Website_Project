Welcome to Ipeleng Cares Foundation!

Our problem is that 3.2 million children are bullied each year in South Africa. More than 67% of children who are bullied will not report it to a teacher because they believe nothing will be done.

The academic implications of bullying include situations in which children who are regularly bullied are three times more likely to miss school and twice as likely to feel excluded from school. They performed poorly on reading and math tests compared to children who were not bullied. We provide solutions such as anti-racism awareness workshops for parents, educators, and learners. We provide anti-bullying policy advice to schools and we also have anti-bullying leaders.
